# Trading Strategies

## Scalping_1

**Timeframe:** M1 (1-minute)

**Indicators:**
- EMA (200) - M15 - trend filter
- RSI (7) - momentum

**Entry Rules:**

**SELL Signal:**
- Price below EMA 200
- RSI > 70 (overbought)
- Bearish engulfing or reversal signal

**BUY Signal:**
- Price above EMA 200  
- RSI < 30 (oversold)
- Bullish engulfing or reversal signal

**Risk Management:**
- Stop loss: Below/above EMA 200
- Take profit: Risk:Reward 1:2 or 1:3

**Note:** Scalping strategy, requires fast execution and low transaction costs.
